## About the Author

**Even Solbraa** is a senior specialist at Equinor ASA and the creator and lead developer of NeqSim (Non-Equilibrium Simulator), an open-source Java toolkit for thermodynamic and process simulation. With over two decades of experience in applied thermodynamics, he has worked extensively on equation of state modeling, process simulation, and digital twin development for oil and gas production facilities on the Norwegian Continental Shelf.

His work on CPA implementation in NeqSim includes multiple solver variants (successive substitution, fully implicit Newton, Broyden quasi-Newton, and Anderson acceleration), comprehensive parameter databases, and integration with full process simulation workflows. He has applied CPA to a wide range of industrial problems including gas dehydration, hydrate prediction, CO$_2$ transport and storage, and produced water management.

Even holds a degree in chemical engineering and has contributed to numerous internal and external publications on thermodynamic modeling and process simulation. He is passionate about making rigorous thermodynamic tools accessible to engineers through open-source software and clear documentation.
